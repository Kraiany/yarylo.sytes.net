You are visiting home page of informal community of Ukrainians in Japan.



  • Helping and developing communications among Ukrainians in Japan;
  • Educating Ukrainians about Japanese culture;
  • Educating Japanese about Ukrainian culture;
  • Helping Ukrainians to adapt to Japanese way of life;
  • Strengthening cultural links between people of two nations.



Mailing list "Kraiany"


Mailing list "Kraiany" devoted to community life of Ukrainians in Japan. Mailing list suggested audience:

  • Ukrainians residing in Japan, both temporarily and permanently;
  • Ukrainians that are interested in Japanese culture and Japanese way of life;
  • Japanese people interested in Ukrainian culture, Ukrainian traditions and way of life;
  • people of other nationalities interested in strengthening and expanding bilateral cultural links between Ukraine and Japan.
